Student Registration Instructions:

To register as a student at our institution, applicants must meet the following eligibility criteria and follow the outlined steps:

Age Requirement:

Applicants must be at least 17 years old as of the date of registration. There is no upper age limit; however, age documentation (such as a birth certificate or government-issued ID) must be provided.

Educational Qualification:

A minimum of successful completion of secondary/high school education (equivalent to 10+2 or Grade 12) is required for undergraduate programs. For postgraduate programs, a recognized bachelor's degree in a relevant field is mandatory.

Required Documents:
  • Proof of identity (e.g., Aadhar card, passport, or national ID)
  • Recent passport-size photograph
  • Academic transcripts and certificates
  • Transfer/migration certificate (if applicable)
  • Caste or community certificate (if applying under reserved category)
Registration Process:
  • Fill out the online or offline registration form completely and accurately.
  • Upload or attach scanned copies of all required documents.
  • Submit the form and pay the non-refundable registration fee, if applicable.
  • Await confirmation via email or SMS with further instructions on admission or entrance tests.
Important Notes:
  • Incomplete forms or incorrect information may lead to disqualification.
  • Original documents must be produced at the time of physical verification or admission.
